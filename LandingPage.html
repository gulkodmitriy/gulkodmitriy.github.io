ABOUT THE X-WING FIGHT

X-Wing Fight is a simple scrolling shooter in Star Wars universe. You are the pilot of X-Wing, fighting with TIE-Fighters and Destroyer ships.
Enjoy it!

GAME CONTROL

Game control is quite simple:

USE ARROW BUTTONS

to move your X-Wing on the game field

USE SPACEBAR
